What are Modes?

Modes are best described as variations of scales. They can be used to convey "feeling" or attitude when writing or improvising, depending on which mode you choose. The most important modes to start with are either the Ionion mode (Major scale) or the Aeolian mode (Minor scale).

How do I know which mode I'm in and should I stay in a mode once I start?

You will know generally by the pattern you will be using and no, you should explore the fretboard utilizing the notes in different modes to convey different feelings or moods
