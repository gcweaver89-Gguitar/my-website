Key of F

What are the notes in the key of F?

F Major (F)


F G A Bb C D E



F Minor (Fm)


F G Ab Bb C Db Eb


You may come across a "b" symbol, this means that the note is a half step Lower than the natural note. You may come across a "b" symbol, this means that the note is a half setp lower than the natural note and will generally be used if you are descending a scale. There are only two sets of notes that will not have a sharp or flat in between them and they are E,F and B,C.
