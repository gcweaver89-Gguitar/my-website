Beginners Guide to Improvisation

Choose your key

Choosing the key to improvise over is a matter of preference, you will soon learn that whichever key you choose is merely the starting note (root). If you are just starting out, the "easiest" to grasp is the key of A.


Once you choose which key you want I will show you the notes for both the Major and Minor variants.This distinction will be more important once we learn about Scales and Modes.





Note: all of the examples for pentatonic patterns and modes are based on standard six string E tuning (E A D G B E)
