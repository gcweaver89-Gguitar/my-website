Key of C

What are the notes in the key of C?

C Major (C)


C D E F G A B



What is unique about the key of C major is that it will have no sharps or flats


C Minor (Cm)


C D Eb/D# F G Ab/G# Bb/A#


You will notice that for this I put both the flat (b) and sharp (#) variants of the notes. This is to help differentiate the notes but they mean the same thing.
